You are the 3. Use the arrow key to imitate the movement of the Es. Once you imitated one whole repetition of an E it disappears and you get an extra life. If you make a mistake you'll loose a life. If you have no lives left the game is over. Oh and there is actually no way to win. New Es will keep spawning and they'll start to move faster.
